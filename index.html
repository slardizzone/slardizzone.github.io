About Me

Hi! My name is Sal Lardizzone, and I'm a Full Stack Web Developer with a passion for creating and building applications using a multitude of different technologies.

There I began dabble with code, which eventually lead to my participation in General Assembly's Web Development Immersive course, located in NYC.


Now most of my time is spent building and improving my current skillset by tackling new technologies every day.
